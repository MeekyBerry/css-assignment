What is 'Audio Desc'?

Audio Desc' or Audio Description is a feature we offer for the visually impaired in some of our cinemas in Northern Ireland. This along with general sound track describes the setting and scenes within the film. You will require a headset which can be obtained at the box office. This does not affect the general viewing of the film. Audio Description films are listed with 'Audio Desc ' beside the show time. If you would like to confirm whether you local Omniplex cinema offers Audio Description headsets, please contact the cinema direct - a full list of contact details can be found here.

What does 15A mean?

Movies classified 15A are considered to have parts of the film which are suitable for those over the age of 15 by the Irish Films Classification Office. However, they can also be seen by a person younger than 15 years provided they are accompanied by an adult over the age of 18.
